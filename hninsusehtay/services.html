Service



Myanmar's community peace and tranquility are attractive and enjoyable for traveling and discovering the virgin destinations around Myanmar, respectively. Phoe wa Travel & Tour Co.,Ltd believes that the goal of business is to create and keep customers. Travel with us, your clients will enjoy the quality experience. Rapid response and having competitive hotels and ground handling rates are our strongest point in this dynamic industry. As such, we can offer you the following services:



Accommodation


We have negotiated specially discounted, contract rates with a large selection of accommodation categories from low-budget 3 star, right through to a range of high-end, exclusive 5 star deluxe hotels. We pride ourselves on having “the most attractive pricing policy” in the region.

VIP Service



Our VIP Service is at your disposal 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. In addition to our outstanding personal service and attention to every client’s individual needs, we offer a unique world class VIP service to ensure our clients enjoy every aspect of luxury travel in true style.

Car Rental Service


You can rent cars on request for trips to major towns in Myanmar for Tours or on Business. The prices are different on trips, days and on daily and monthly use. Prices also depend on the types and models of vehicles and local registration numbers. Latest vehicles and registration numbers usually charge more, but in any case it is cheaper to hire for long term use. You may be able to hire a car with experience driver from us.

Flight Services


We have very professional and experienced team always at your disposal and ready to arrange every element of your holiday including flights, transfers, excursion, tours and any other additional services. You can be assured that from arrival to departure your holiday will be tailored to your needs.

Business Travel & Corporate Travel


We adapt our Business Travel and Corporate Travel services to suit the needs of each client. We have experience working with international companies as well as small, mid-sized and large organizations as the best corporate travel partner available in Myanmar.

Transportation / Transfers


Our fleet is used for all kinds of transfers and customized services including tailor-made solutions for airport transfers, complete organization of all transport requirements for meetings, congresses, incentive trips and excursions, as well as group handling. It consists of a selection of luxury owned and leased vehicles as well as limousines driven by friendly, polite experienced, tourism educated drivers.


Excursions


Our team is available to further ensure and provide your excursions through this unique country by arranging for all interests, adjusted to the features of each individual market. Excursions whether by plane, boat, or luxury coach are led by skilled and experienced tour guides whose total aim and focus is to professionally cover every aspect of interest in each destination.
